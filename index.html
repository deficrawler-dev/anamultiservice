How fast can Ana get someone out to me?
Most requests get a same-day response, and urgent issues like storm damage or a burst pipe are treated as priority. Reach out by call or WhatsApp and Ana will tell you exactly what to expect before anyone shows up.
Do you handle emergencies?
Yes. Burst pipes, storm-damaged roofs, and the aftermath of a car accident don't wait for business hours, so Ana doesn't either. Message any time and you'll hear back fast.
Is there a fee just to get a quote?
No. Initial consultations for roofing and plumbing are free, with no pressure to commit. You'll get an honest read on the problem and your options before any money changes hands.
What happens after a car accident?
Ana becomes your advocate from the first call — walking you through next steps, connecting you with the right resources, and staying on your side while things get sorted out.
Do you only work in Sandy Springs?
Sandy Springs is home base, but Ana also serves the surrounding metro Atlanta area. If you're nearby and not sure you're covered, just ask — it takes one message to find out.
How do I actually get started?
Call or WhatsApp — whichever is easier. Tell Ana what's going on and she'll point you toward the right help, whether that's roofing, plumbing, or support after an accident.
